INTRODUCTION

Welcome to Shoorveer Academy! Founded on February 17, 2021, by visionary leader Mangwana Manish Kumar, Shoorveer Academy is dedicated to empowering the next generation of leaders, innovators, and changemakers. Our name, derived from the Sanskrit word meaning "Extreme Warrior", embodies our mission to cultivate individuals who are Unbreakable, Unbeatable, and Unstoppable in their pursuit of excellence. Shoorveer Academy is more than just a martial arts institution; it's a journey of self-discovery, empowerment, and transformation.

ABOUT ACADEMY

    AT SHOORVEER ACADEMY, WE OFFER A COMPREHENSIVE CURRICULUM THAT ENCOMPASSES KICKBOXING, GRAPPLING, MIXED MARTIAL ARTS (MMA), SELF-DEFENSE, CROSSFIT, FITNESS TRAINING AND YOGA. WITH TOP-NOTCH FACILITIES AND DEDICATED FACULTY MEMBERS, WE PROVIDE AN ENVIRONMENT WHERE STUDENTS CAN THRIVE ACADEMICALLY, PHYSICALLY, AND PERSONALLY.

  • Kickboxing: Master the art of striking and defense techniques in a dynamic and high-energy environment.
  • Grappling: Learn ground fighting and submission strategies through structured training.
  • MMA: Experience a powerful fusion of various martial arts to develop all-around fighting skills.
  • Self-Defense: Equip yourself with practical skills to protect yourself and others in any situation.
  • CrossFit Training: Boost strength, agility, and endurance through functional fitness training.
  • Yoga: Find balance, flexibility, and inner peace with ancient practices for both body and soul.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to nurture the Shoorveer spirit within each student, fostering resilience, determination, and a commitment to excellence. Through holistic education and personalized mentorship, we aim to empower our students to become leaders in their chosen fields and make a positive impact on society.
