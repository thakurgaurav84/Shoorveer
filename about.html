INTRODUCTION

Welcome to Shoorveer Academy! Founded on February 17, 2021, by Mangwana Manish Kumar, Shoorveer Academy was born from a deep passion to create a space where discipline, resilience, and strength converge to shape extraordinary individuals. Our very name, drawn from the Sanskrit word meaning "Extreme Warrior", reflects our core philosophy — to forge a community of warriors who are Unbreakable, Unbeatable, and Unstoppable in every aspect of life.

At Shoorveer Academy, we believe that martial arts is not merely a sport, but a powerful tool for personal growth and transformation. Here, we blend traditional values with modern training techniques, ensuring that every student develops not only physical prowess but also mental clarity, emotional balance, and unwavering confidence.

Our programs go beyond kicks, punches, and grapples — they instill the virtues of discipline, respect, perseverance, and self-mastery. We prepare our students to face challenges both inside and outside the arena, empowering them to become leaders, innovators, and changemakers in their communities.

ABOUT ACADEMY

At Shoorveer Academy, we pride ourselves on delivering a comprehensive and world-class curriculum that blends physical mastery, mental resilience, and personal growth. Our programs span across Kickboxing, Grappling, Mixed Martial Arts (MMA), Self-Defense, CrossFit, Fitness Training, and Yoga — ensuring every student develops into a well-rounded and confident individual.

With state-of-the-art facilities, experienced trainers, and a supportive community, we provide an environment where students can push boundaries, achieve their goals, and discover their true potential. Whether you aspire to compete at the highest level, improve your fitness, or gain practical self-defense skills, Shoorveer Academy offers the tools, guidance, and motivation you need to excel.

Our Key Training Disciplines:

Kickboxing:

Master powerful strikes, defensive maneuvers, and footwork in a high-energy and motivating atmosphere. Perfect for building speed, strength, and stamina while sharpening your reflexes.

Grappling:

Learn the science of ground control, submissions, and escapes. Our structured training equips you with effective techniques for both sport and self-defense situations.

Mixed Martial Arts (MMA):

Experience the fusion of multiple martial arts disciplines to develop all-around combat skills. From striking to clinch work to ground fighting, our MMA training creates complete and adaptable fighters.

Self-Defense:

Gain practical, real-world protection skills that prepare you for unexpected situations. Our self-defense program focuses on awareness, prevention, and quick, decisive action.

CrossFit & Functional Fitness:

Build total-body strength, agility, and endurance with intense yet scalable workouts designed to push your physical limits while preventing injuries.

Yoga & Mindfulness:

Restore balance to your body and mind through stretching, breathing techniques, and meditation. Yoga at Shoorveer Academy complements our high-intensity training by enhancing flexibility, focus, and inner peace.

At Shoorveer Academy, training is more than just exercise — it's a journey of discipline, confidence, and transformation. We are committed to molding champions both inside and outside the ring, empowering every individual to live with strength, courage, and purpose.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to ignite and nurture the Shoorveer spirit within every student — a spirit rooted in courage, resilience, and an unyielding drive for excellence. We believe that true strength is built not only through physical skill, but also through mental toughness, emotional balance, and moral integrity.

At Shoorveer Academy, we go beyond traditional training methods, offering holistic education that addresses the mind, body, and spirit. Every student receives personalized mentorship, ensuring that their unique strengths are recognized, developed, and sharpened to their fullest potential.
